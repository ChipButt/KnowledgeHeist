How to Play

On a computer: Use the arrow keys to move. Press Enter to grab. You can also click and hold on the game area to move Nana. On a mobile: Drag on the game area to move Nana. Use the GRAB button to attempt to steal items. Walk Nana around the museum. Move up to an artwork or floor item and attempt to grab it. Each item triggers a question. Get it right and you steal it. Get it wrong and it greys out and you get a strike. Three strikes brings in the guard. If you make it to the exit, you escape with your haul. Questions only get used once until you reset your progress.
