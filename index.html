How to Play

You are the brains behind a heist syndicate with buyers ready to pay well for anything lifted from the Museum This Museum has never been hit before because their smart security, but are you more clevererer than it? The Job: Move through the museum and approach an item to attempt the grab Answer the Security Question correctly to successfully nab it Get it wrong and the item gets locked down Trigger too many lock downs and you might alert the guard When you are happy with your haul, get to the exit! Computer Use the arrow keys or click and hold on the game area to move Press Enter to grab Mobile Drag on the game area to move Tap the floating GRAB prompt when it appears near an item
