How to Play

You’re the mastermind now. We’ve got a museum that’s never been cracked—until tonight. The item locks down, and too many mistakes will have the guards breathing down your neck. Once you’ve snagged enough, slip out unnoticed. Just remember, this is your shot at a legendary haul, but if you're not smart about it, you could get caught! Playing on Computer?
